Materials, Synthesis, and Characterization Platform

About the Platform

Our research center offers advanced spectroscopic analysis, including NMR, IR, and UV-Vis, along with expertise in chemical, structural, mechanical, physico-chemical, and metallographic analyses. With four cutting-edge laboratories, we drive innovation, support collaborative research, and provide training for academic and industrial partners.

Our Expertise

We assess parameters including crystallinity, surface area, morphology, chemical composition, particle size, density, and moisture content, along with mechanical properties like elasticity, hardness, toughness, and fatigue resistance. Our tailored analyses provide detailed spectra and clear reports to enhance understanding and support scientific and industrial development.

Powder Characterization:

Involves analyzing powders to understand their physical and chemical properties:

Particle Size:

Measures size distribution affecting flow and mixing.

Surface Properties:

Assesses surface area and texture, influencing reactivity and adhesion.

Performance Insights:

Evaluates stability and effectiveness, including moisture content.



Mechanical Characterization:

Assesses materials response to stress, focusing on properties like elasticity, hardness, toughness, and fatigue resistance to gauge performance.

Material Response to Stress:

Assesses how materials react when subjected to various forces or loads

Elasticity:

Measures the materials ability to return to its original shape after deformation.

Hardness:

Evaluates the material's resistance to surface deformation or indentation.

Toughness:

Determines the material's ability to absorb energy and withstand impact before fracturing.

Fatigue Resistance:

Analyzes how the material endures repeated stress cycles without failing.
